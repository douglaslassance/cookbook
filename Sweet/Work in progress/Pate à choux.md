Water = 250 g (Optionally, half of the water can be swapped with milk)  
Butter = 40% of Water  
Flour = 80% of Water  
Eggs = 80% of Water  
Salt = 2% of Water    

- Bring water, butter, and salt to a simmer.
- Mix in the flour until the mixture does not stick to the pan.
- Let cool down and add incorporate the eggs one by one.
- Put the resulting batter in a pastry bag.
- Make the desired shapes on a tray and cook for 30 minutes at 180°C.
